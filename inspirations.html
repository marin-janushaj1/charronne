INSPIRATIONS

The Vision

This collection is built on the idea that each step is a performance — footwear becomes a sculptural extension of the stage. The mind map explores a world where theater, surrealism, and luxury craftsmanship intersect: red velvet drapes and spotlight tulle evoke curtain movement, while gold sculptural heels, moon shapes, and pleated volumes take inspiration from the theatres surrealist language.

The palette of black leather, deep red velvet, and gilded gold creates dramatic contrasts reminiscent of opera houses and masked performances. This concept positions the wearer as a commanding presence: powerful, mysterious, and expressive. The shoes do not simply accessorize — they act, embodying narrative, character, and visual rhythm with every silhouette.

Luxurious Materials

Every piece in our collection is crafted from the finest materials, meticulously selected for their exceptional quality and luxurious feel. From sumptuous leathers and rich velvets to delicate silks and gleaming metals, each material tells a story of elegance, craftsmanship, and theatrical grandeur.

Theatrical Grandeur

The rich burgundy of theatre curtains, the warm glow of stage lights, and the intricate gold detailing of opera house balconies inspire our luxurious color palette. Each garment is designed to make its wearer the star of their own performance, commanding attention with understated drama.

Ballet's Grace

The fluid movements of ballet dancers—their strength, discipline, and ethereal beauty—inform the way our fabrics drape and flow. Like a tutu's perfect layers or a dancer's extended line, our silhouettes celebrate both structure and movement, power and delicacy.

Baroque Artistry

Ornate frames adorned with golden flourishes, celestial motifs like the crescent moon, and the architectural magnificence of centuries-old theatres—these baroque elements translate into our intricate details, luxurious embellishments, and commitment to timeless craftsmanship.

Charonne crafts restrained opulence—quiet luxury for those who appreciate artistry in every detail.
